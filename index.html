The Waypost

A Landmark in Fine Dining.

Waypost – A Journey in Every Meal

Founded in 2012, Waypost is a family-run restaurant built on the belief that every meal is a journey. Rooted in a passion for seasonal, locally sourced ingredients, our menu reflects a deep respect for both tradition and innovation. Our kitchen crafts dishes with care, blending bold flavors with refined techniques to create something both familiar and unexpected. Set in a warm, inviting space, Waypost is a place to gather, unwind, and savor—whether you're celebrating or simply seeking a memorable meal.

The Waypost Menu – Crafted with Care, Inspired by Season

Charred Heritage Carrots

Fire-kissed heirloom carrots, draped in velvety tahini yogurt, finished with smoked honey and toasted pistachios for a perfect balance of earthiness and sweetness.

Dry-Aged Ribeye with Black Garlic Butter

Seared to perfection, our 30-day dry-aged ribeye is bathed in umami-rich black garlic butter and served alongside crisp rosemary potatoes and charred broccolini.

Seared Scallops & Corn Purée

Plump, golden-seared scallops rest on a silky bed of sweet corn purée, accented with crispy pancetta, pickled shallots, and a drizzle of chili oil.

Wild Mushroom & Truffle Risotto

Creamy Arborio rice, slow-cooked with foraged mushrooms, finished with shaved black truffle, aged Parmesan, and a whisper of white truffle oil.

Dark Chocolate & Sea Salt Tart

A luscious dark chocolate ganache nestled in a crisp cocoa crust, topped with flakes of Maldon sea salt and a dollop of espresso cream for the perfect sweet-salty indulgence.

Today's Special

Saffron-Poached Halibut with Spring Pea Velouté

Delicately poached in a golden saffron broth, our line-caught halibut melts in your mouth, balanced by a velvety spring pea velouté and a crisp citrus gremolata. Finished with first-of-the-season morel mushrooms, this limited-time dish captures the freshest flavors of early spring—available only while these peak ingredients last.
